Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? Tourist Guide AI tells you everything.

General Questions

How does Tourist Guide AI create my route?
Using your current location, date, and your customized profile, our AI instantly creates personalized itineraries with hidden gems, realistic timings and directions, all tailored to you.
Do I need to create an account?
No account needed to start! You can pay anonymously and use the app instantly. Creating an account (or linking later) lets you keep your data forever across devices.
Is the information always accurate?
Our AI pulls from millions of real traveler reviews and up-to-date sources. While extremely accurate, prices and opening hours can change. Always double-check critical info (especially transport and visas).

Subscriptions & Payments

How much does it cost?
Unlimited access costs only $2.99–$29.99 per trip (depending on length), paid once via App Store or Google Play. No monthly fees, no hidden charges.
Will my subscription auto-renew?
No. Every plan is one-time per trip. You only pay again if you want unlimited access for a new destination.

Data & Privacy

Do you sell my data?
Never. Your trips, photos, and location data are private and are never shared or sold to third parties.
How do I delete my account and all data?

If you used the app anonymously (no email added), simply deleting the app removes everything permanently — we never stored anything on our servers.

If you created an account with email, you can permanently delete your account and all associated data here → Delete my account .

This action is immediate and irreversible.
